Bernard Spilsbury

  • Born:      May 16, 1867
  • Died:      December 17, 1947 (age 70)
  • Spouse:   Edith Caroline Horton
  • Nationality:   British
  • Career:   Forensic Pathologist

Sir Spilsbury studied natural science at the Magdalen College in Oxford, later specializing in forensic pathology.

Read more here...

After graduating with a degree in natural science, Spilsbury retained an assistance position at St. Mary's Hospital in London. This position allowed him to gain valuable experience in post-mortem pathology. This experience lead to many contributions in the field of Forensic Pathology and Criminal Investigations.
